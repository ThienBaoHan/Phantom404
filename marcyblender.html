Marceline Blender Model

    Hello guys. This is my first model I created in blender. To be honest, I was very suprised how simple it was to scuplt a 3D model in Blender, I always thought such thing would take many years to learn, but all it took was following one hour tutorial long. (Many thanks to CG BOOST!)

    Of course I'm not saying that learning Blender is easy, it takes years and constant learning to master the program, my point is just give it a shot and you'll see, it is not as intimidating as it seems to be.


About this Model

    As the name suggested, this 3D model was based on Marceline Abadeer, (aka The Vampire Queen), one of the main characters in Adventure Time. Also the whole reason why I started learning Blender (You have no idea how much I wanted Marceline in action with Arcane's art style!!).
